## 10. Optical Fibers

### ğŸ”¹ Definition:
An optical fiber is a thin, flexible strand of glass or plastic that carries light using total internal reflection.

### ğŸ”¹ Structure:
- **Core:** Inner part, where light travels
- **Cladding:** Outer layer with lower refractive index
- **Jacket:** Protective outer layer

### ğŸ”¹ Working:
Light entering the fiber undergoes repeated total internal reflections and moves through the fiber.

### ğŸ”¹ Applications:
- High-speed internet and telecommunication
- Endoscopy in medical field
- Light transmission in instruments
